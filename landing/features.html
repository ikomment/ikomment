Every feature. Nothing paywalled.

The complete list — what your readers see, what site owners control, and the engineering working silently underneath.

💬 Comments

  • Deeply nested replies, unlimited depth
  • Collapsible threads; deep chains flatten into “Continue thread →”
  • Live updates — no page refresh
  • Up/down votes with instant feedback
  • Edit & delete your own (owner-set time window)
  • Markdown: bold, italic, code, safe links
  • Optional formatting toolbar (off by default)
  • Sorting: Top · Newest · Oldest
  • Pinned comments
  • Report/flag on every comment
  • Comment-count badges for listings
  • Optimistic posting — comments appear instantly

❓ Q&A

  • Question + answers format, separate from chatter
  • Accepted answer pinned to top with ✅
  • Answers ranked by votes; Top/Newest filter
  • Nested comment discussion under each answer
  • Answered / unanswered status at a glance

🗣️ Forums & live chat

  • Owner-managed categories/boards
  • Visitor-created threads with titles
  • Activity-sorted thread lists with reply counts
  • 📌 Pinned and 🔒 locked threads
  • Member profiles: avatar, joined date, recent posts
  • Real-time chat room per category
  • Presence counter (“3 people here”)
  • Chat history survives between visits

🔒 Identity — five ways in, all owner toggles

  • Guest posting — just a name; off by default, typed confirmation to enable; returning guests are remembered
  • Email sign-in links — no passwords; works even if the link is opened on another device; Resend or SendGrid, your choice
  • Google and GitHub — one-tap popup sign-in
  • SSO — your existing logged-in users comment seamlessly; one-page integration, cryptographically signed

🛡️ Moderation

  • AI spam detection — free, every plan
  • Pre-moderation with automatic trust levels — proven commenters skip the queue, invisibly
  • Approve/spam straight from your email inbox
  • Word filters: hold for review or block outright
  • Shadow bans — banned users never know
  • One-click nuke: erase a user's entire history
  • Swipe-to-moderate on mobile
  • Complete audit trail of every action, human or AI

🎨 Customisation & control

  • Auto dark/light — reads your site's actual colours
  • Accent colour, font, corner roundness — live preview
  • Adapts to its own container width (sidebars included)
  • Fully fluid: 320px phones to ultrawide monitors
  • Every feature is a dashboard toggle
  • Per-page overrides (e.g. comments off on one article)
  • Typed confirmation guards consequential settings
  • Changes go live on your site within seconds

📥 Migration

  • One-click Disqus import — upload the export straight from your email, even still zipped
  • Nesting, names, emails and timestamps preserved; Disqus spam left behind
  • Runs in your browser with a progress bar — works from a phone

⚙️ The invisible greatness — what works in the dark

Things you'll never click on, all working on every single comment:

1.1KB loader

Visitors download a tiny loader; each module fetches lazily, only on pages that use it, just before it's scrolled into view.

Pre-rendered at the edge

Comments are converted to ready-to-display HTML server-side and cached at the network edge nearest each reader. No spinners.

Injection-proof

Every comment is sanitised before storage — script tags, event handlers and hostile links are neutralised. Imported content too.

Signed email links

Moderation and sign-in links are cryptographically signed and expire — a forwarded or tampered link is worthless.

Failure-proof AI

If the spam model is ever unavailable, comments fall back to your normal moderation rules. An AI outage can never silence your site.

Honest vote counting

One vote per person per post, enforced by the database itself — counters update by difference and can't drift.

Secrets stay secret

API keys live as deployment secrets, never in the database — a data leak can't expose your email or login credentials.

Built to travel

Data access flows through a platform adapter, so the planned Docker version swaps one layer — not the product.

Accountable by design

Every moderation action — by you, or by the AI — is written to a permanent audit log.
