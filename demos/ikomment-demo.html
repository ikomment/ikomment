The Quiet Comeback of the Personal Blog

A demo article · iKomment test page

After a decade of social feeds, something interesting is happening: people are drifting back to small, personal corners of the web. Places where the writing is slower, the design is theirs, and the conversation happens right under the post.

That conversation is what this page demonstrates. Scroll down — the comment widget below isn't a screenshot. It's the real iKomment comments module, running against a simulated backend, so every feature works: nested replies, votes, editing, sign-in (Google, GitHub, and the new Email login — simulated instantly), sorting, collapsing threads.

📱 Demo mode — comments live in this page's memory only and reset on refresh. The real version talks to the Cloudflare Worker API. Resize the window (or rotate your phone) to watch the widget adapt fluidly.
